LockerShelf

The Story

The locker shelf market is saturated with products that are poor quaility and don't fit standard locker sizes. In highschool I designed a new Lockershelf that uses the walls of the locker to create a strong seamless fit. After selling hundreds of units locally the company has grown to selling 50,000+ units nationally.

The product line has expended, the design has been refined through several product iterations, and production moved from wood to injection molding. LockersShelf has become a part-time family business that ships thousands of units during our peak months. Without advertising The Lockershelf Company has seen double digit year of year growth.

I co-hold a utility patent.

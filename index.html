The Lanutrnal

Arduino based lighting system that lights the room inversely to the suns diurnal cycle.

How it Works

As the sun moves across the sky the opposite areas of the room are lit. This pattern continues until high noon when all lights are off as the sun is at the brightest point. At sunset the final light turns on and all lights remain on during nightfall. Lights also flash the hour on the hour.

Next Steps

Connect system to internet to relate lighting to weather hue and phone notifications
